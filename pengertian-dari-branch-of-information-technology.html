6. ada yang bisa jelasin iniTechnology development: basic technology x Technology application: technology + application x Application launch: technology + application + product launch x Application growth: technology + application + product sales x Technology maturity: technology + application + fall in product sales x Degraded technology: minimal product sales + loss of application + alternative technology​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan untuk setiap istilah dalam konteks perkembangan teknologi:

1. Technology development (Pengembangan Teknologi):

Ini merujuk pada proses pengembangan teknologi dasar atau fondasi teknologi yang baru. Ini melibatkan penelitian, eksperimen, dan inovasi untuk menciptakan teknologi baru atau meningkatkan teknologi yang sudah ada.

2. Technology application (Penerapan Teknologi):

Ini merujuk pada penggunaan teknologi dalam konteks nyata atau praktis. Penerapan teknologi melibatkan mengintegrasikan teknologi ke dalam berbagai bidang atau industri untuk meningkatkan efisiensi, produktivitas, atau memenuhi kebutuhan spesifik.

3. Application launch (Peluncuran Aplikasi):

Ini merujuk pada tahap dimana teknologi dan penerapannya dikembangkan menjadi produk yang dapat diluncurkan ke pasar. Ini melibatkan proses produksi, pemasaran, dan peluncuran produk yang menggunakan teknologi tersebut.

4. Application growth (Pertumbuhan Aplikasi):

Ini merujuk pada tahap dimana aplikasi teknologi telah diluncurkan dan mulai mendapatkan penjualan yang signifikan. Pada tahap ini, produk yang menggunakan teknologi tersebut mengalami pertumbuhan dalam hal penjualan dan adopsi oleh pengguna.

5. Technology maturity (Maturitas Teknologi):

Ini merujuk pada tahap dimana teknologi dan aplikasinya telah mencapai tingkat kematangan yang tinggi. Pada tahap ini, produk yang menggunakan teknologi tersebut telah mencapai puncak popularitasnya dan penjualan mulai menurun karena adanya persaingan atau munculnya teknologi alternatif.

6. Degraded technology (Teknologi yang Menurun):

Ini merujuk pada tahap dimana teknologi yang digunakan sudah tidak lagi populer atau relevan. Penjualan produk yang menggunakan teknologi tersebut menurun secara signifikan, aplikasi teknologi tersebut hilang atau kehilangan daya tarik, dan alternative teknologi mulai menggantikan posisinya di pasar.

Demikianlah penjelasan untuk setiap istilah dalam konteks perkembangan teknologi yang diberikan.

25. write information about the technology below! dalam bentuk teks report


Wi-Fi is a technology that utilizes electronic equipment to exchange data wirelessly (using radio waves) through a computer network, including high-speed Internet connections. The Wi-Fi Alliance defines Wi-Fi as "any wireless local area network (WLAN) product based on the standards of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. However, because most WLANs today are based on these standards, the term" Wi-Fi "is used in general English as a "WLAN" synonym.

A device that can use Wi-Fi (such as personal computers, video game consoles, smartphones, tablets or digital audio players) can be connected to network sources such as the Internet through a wireless network access point. Such an access point (or hotspot) has a range of about 20 meters (65 feet) indoors and wider outdoors. Hotspot coverage can cover an area as large as a room with walls that block radio waves or several square miles - this can be done using several overlapping access points.

"Wi-Fi" is a Wi-Fi Alliance trademark and brand name for products that use a standard family. Only Wi-Fi products that complete the Wi-Fi Alliance interoperability certification trial may use the name and trademark "Wi-Fi CERTIFIED".

Wi-Fi has a changing security history. The first encryption system, WEP, proved to be easily penetrated. Higher quality protocols, WPA and WPA2, are then added. However, an optional feature added in 2007 called Wi-Fi Protected Setup (WPS), has a gap that allows an attacker to get a WPA or WPA2 router password remotely in a few hours. Some companies recommend turning off the WPS feature. The Wi-Fi Alliance has since renewed its testing plan and certification program to ensure all newly certified equipment is immune from harsh AP PIN attacks.

27. Advantage and disadvantage of technology​


Advantages and Disadvantages of Technology

What is technology? Technology usually refers to the tools and machines that are developed to solve real-world problems. Today we use technology for almost everything, from mundane things like cooking to complex problems like outer space missions. However, like everything else, there are advantages and disadvantages to technology.

One evident advantage of technology is in the medical field. Medical technology has help treat sick people, save lives, and fight viruses and bacteria. The invention of the telephone, the computer, and later, the internet is also an important plus of technology. It enhances communications. Now we can easily communicate and do business with people from all corners of the world. Technology in industry makes productions faster. Workers can produce more goods in less time.

On the other hand, technology also has its drawbacks. Man becomes depending too much on technology. For example the calculator makes students lazy and not exercising their memory. This decreases the brainpower; makes people less smart. Modern weapons cause deaths and suffering of millions of people. Vehicles use fuel that harms the environment. The internet is rampant with hoaxes and propaganda that may lead to riots and anarchy.

In conclusion, while technology is indeed important and beneficial, we should also be wary of its bad aspects. Technology can improve our life but it also can ruin it. We must use it wisely.

Text organization of report are as follows:

a). General classification (introduces the topic of the report/tells
