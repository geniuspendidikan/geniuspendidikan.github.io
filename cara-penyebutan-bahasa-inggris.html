10. Sebutkan cara membuat nasi kuning serta terjemahkan ke dalam bahasa inggrisnya


-3 Tbsp. butter

-1 c. long-grain white rice

-1 tsp fresh minced garlic

-1 tsp. onion powder or dried minced onion

-1 tsp. ground turmeric

-2 c. chicken broth

-1/2 tsp. Kosher salt or to taste

ground black pepper to taste

-2-3 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS

1.Heat butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.

2.Add the rice and gently stir until rice begins to lightly brown.

3.Add garlic, onion powder, and turmeric and stir until well mixed in.

4.Slowly stir in chicken broth and raise heat to medium-high. Add salt and pepper to taste.

5.Bring the mix to a boil then turn the heat to low and cover the pan with a lid.

6.Simmer for 20 to 25 minutes. Remove from heat and add cilantro, and fluff with a fork. Serves 4.

7.Finally,you can serve the yellow rice!

18. sebutkan langkah-langkah cara menulis dalam bahasa inggris


1. Topic Selection and Determination
Choose and set the topic of an important first step, because there is no writing without something to write about. The first problem faced by the writer to formulate the theme of an essay is the topic or subject matter (Keraf, 1993: 126). In choosing and placing this topic there is a need for skills or knowledge or sincerity.

2. Determine Writing Objectives and Writings
The purpose of writing is defined as a pattern that controls writing as a whole (Akhadiah, 1998: 89). By determining the purpose of writing, it is known what to do at the writing stage, even what is needed, the scope of the discussion, organization, and possibly the point of view used. Explicitly, the purpose of writing can be stated in a thesis way or by stating intent.

3. Writing Material
Writing material is all information or data used to achieve the writing objectives. The material may be in the form of details, case history, examples, explanations, definitions, facts, causal relationships, results of testing hypotheses, numbers, diagrams, pictures, etc. (Akhadiah, 1998: 90).

4. Arranging the Framework of Writings
An essay contains a work plan, containing the main provisions on how a topic must be detailed and developed. The essay guarantees a logical and orderly arrangement, and allows an author to distinguish the main ideas from additional ideas.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban:

Penulisan tanggal lahir pada bahasa inggris dibagi menjadi 2 jenis, yaitu:

British Style: Day, Month, Year

Contohnya: 3rd January 2020 (03/01/20)

American Style: Month, Day, Year

Contohnya: January 3rd 2020 (01/03/20)

Untuk sebelum tahun 2000, dibaca dengan cara membaca dua angka awal lalu dua angka akhir, contohnya:

1945: Nineteen forty five

1998: Nineteen ninty eight

1999: Nineteen ninty nine

Untuk Setelah tahun 2000, Cara membacanya seperti membaca angka ribuan pada biasanya, contohnya:

2001: Two thousand and one

2002: Two thousand and two

2021: Two thousand and twenty one

Contoh tulisan dan cara membacanya:

1st December 2021: The first of December, Two thousand and twenty one (01/12/21)

17rd January 1963: The seventeenth of January, Nineteen sisxty three (17/01/1963)

34. Sebutkan cara membuat rengginang dalam bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:

Ingredient:

1.) 400 grams of good quality white glutinous rice

2.) 200 ml water

3.) 1 tsp fine granulated sugar

Ground spices:

1.) 4 cloves garlic

2.) 1/3 tsp round pepper

3.) 1/2 tsp grilled shrimp paste

4.) 1 tbsp salt

Steps to make rengginang:

1. Wash the glutinous rice, soak it in water for three hours. Drain.

2. Steam the glutinous rice until half cooked for 20 minutes or until the rice grains start to stick. Remove, transfer to a saucepan.

3. Bring water to a boil, add sugar and ground spices, stir. After boiling, remove the glutinous rice immediately.

4. Pour the cooked spices into a pot of glutinous rice, mix well. Cover the pot, let stand about 30 minutes or until the water is completely absorbed by the sticky rice. Steam the glutinous rice again until cooked, about 30 minutes.

5. Take one tablespoon of sticky rice that is still hot, flatten it into a round shape with a diameter of 6 cm and a thickness of 0.5 cm.

Place on a clean dry tray but do not stack. Do this until all the glutinous rice is used up.

6. Dry in the sun for about three days or until completely dry. Store in a dry place.

7. Heat enough cooking oil over high heat. Fry the renggingan while turning it over until the glutinous rice grains start to expand. Lift and drain.
