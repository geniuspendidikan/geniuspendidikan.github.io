20. The next terms of the sequence 1,5,11,19....is.....​


The question is about Un.
b = U2 - U1 = 5 - 1 = 4
b = U3 - U2 = 11 - 5 = 6
b = U4 - U3 = 19 - 11 = 8
Setiap jaraknya +2

Untuk mencari U5, U6 dan U7 menggunakan rumus Un.
Un = a + (n - 1) b
U5 = 1 + 4b
U5 = 1 + 4(7)
U5 = 29

U6 = 1 + 5b
U6 = 1 + 5(8)
U6 = 41

U7 = 1 + 6b
U7 = 1 + 6(9)
U7 = 55

Deretnya adalah
1,5,9,11,19,29,41,55.

Jawaban:

2. The children played kites this afternoon.

3. The children will play kits next week.

Penjelasan:

Present:

Some sentence that begin with Everyday, every morning and so on and the subject is he/she/it use s at the verb. (use only if you are doing it regullarly)

If the word doesn't have the subject he/she/it DON'T use s.

If you are doing the verb now use ing.

Past:

Use ed at the back of the word.

If the word end in y change it into i.

Future:

Use will before the verb.

If you take part in a game or a sport, you play it. You might love to play charades, but no one is going to want to play with you if you keep forgetting the rules. The verb play also means to make music, like when you play your ukulele. And if you toy with a concept — like forming a ukulele orchestra — you play around with the idea.

"Engage in recreational activities rather than work; occupy oneself in a diversion."
