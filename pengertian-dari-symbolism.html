EXPLANATION

1. Personification

A figure of speech in which the inanimate objects, things, animals are connected with the living qualities, the human traits or they have an ability like the human does.

2. Hyperbole

An exaggerative statement for emphasis. In Bahasa, we assume it as the statement "lebay."

3. Simile  

A comparison made between two different things but at least there is a point in common. It’s commonly identified by the words “like”, “as”, “or.”

4. Symbolism

A word, an image that reveals  complex values, abstract ideas.  Symbol is defined as something that has the wide meaning and it is influenced by a cultural concept.

BASED ON YOUR QUESTIONS ABOVE

The first thing you do is translating the statements into the understandable meaning and pay attention to each meaning of figure of speech. So, you can easily solve them, and imply for the meaning of figure of speech hidden in the statement.

1. Our team wins. The other team waves the white flag [Tim sepak bola kami menang. Tim lain mengibarkan bendera putih]

➡ This statement uses symbolism. The white flag has a meaning, a surrender, losing.

2. I have a ton of homework.[Saya mengerjakan berton-ton PR]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "hyperbole." It emphasizes the amount of homework she/he does (lots of homework).

3 Her hair was as dark as night. [Rambutnya selegam malam]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "simile." It compares two distinctive things but there is a point in common, her black hair color looks like the night sky.

4. The cookies begged me to eat them [Kue-kue itu memohon kepada sy untuk dimakan]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "personification." The inanimate thing "cookie" has an ability like the human does "begging."

5. I'll finish doing the dishes faster than you can blink. [Sy akn menyelesaikan mencuci piring secepat kamu berkedip]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "hyperbole." It emphasizes an attempt.

6. The rain gently pat my head [Hujan ini menepuk kepala saya dengan lembutnya]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "personification." The inanimate thing "rain" acts like the human does "pat."

7. My coffee was hot like lava. [Kopi sy panasnya seperti lahar]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "simile." It compares two distinctive things "coffee and lava", but at least there is a point in common.

8. The stars winked at me. [Bintang-bintang itu berkedip kepada sy]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "personification." The inanimate thing "star" has an ability like the human does "wink."

9. I ate so much. I think I'm going to explode.

[Sy makan terlalu banyak. Sy pikir sy akan meledak]

➡ This statement uses figure of speech "hyperbole." It doesn't literally mean she/he will explode like the volcano but it means she/he feels full.

10. I'm feeling so blue. [Sy merasa biru]

➡ This statement uses symbolism. Feeling blue is an idiomatic symbol, connected to the sadness or depression.

Topik

Untuk menentukan topik, kita harus mencari main idea di setiap paragraf.

"So amazing was the structure of the Great Pyramid despite its antiquity that it caused many experts to scratch their heads as to ask about who might have been this superstructure’s builders.

Bukti:

"Further scientific study indicates that these represent a type of timeline of events—past, present, and future."

⇒ Jadi, jelas jawabannya C. Wonders of the Great Pyramid of Giza, atau Keajaiban Piramida Agung Giza

Main Idea

Untuk menentukan main idea, menggunakan skimming skills (membaca cepat menemukan kata kunci/ idea) sama seperti menentukan topik. Purpose

Extra-terrestrial

25. "world keeps turning." what figurative language is used in the sentence?a. Hyperbole b. Simile c. Symbolism d. Idiom e. Personification​


The figurative language used in the sentence "world keeps turning" is personification. (E)

Personification is a figure of speech in which human qualities or characteristics are attributed to non-human things. In this case, the non-human thing, which is the world, is being attributed with the human quality of "turning", which is something only living beings can do.
