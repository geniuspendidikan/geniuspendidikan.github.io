7. possitive negative interogative simple present tense


(+) she reads some book every day
(-) she doesn't read some book every day
(?) Does she read some book every day?

be best..1. Simple Present bentuk Verbal (ada kata kerjanya);

(+) I play a ball every evening
(-) I do not play a ball every evening
(?) Do I play a ball every evening ?

*verb (kata kerja) = play
*time signal (waktu yg menunjukkan simple present)
= every, usually, sometimes dll

2. Simple Present bentuk Nominal (tak ada kata kerja);

(+) I am a student
(-) I am not a student
(?) am I a student?

rumusnya
#simple present form
*Verbal Sentence
(+) subject + verb1(-s/-es) + object
(-) subject + do/does + not + verb1 + object
(?) do/does + subject + verb 1 + object?

*Nominal sentence
(+) subject + to be (is, am, are) + object
(-) subject + to be (is, am, are) + not + object
(?) to be + subject + object?

Simple Present biasanya digunakan utk menunjukkan waktu sekarang yg bersifat umum atau sebuah rutinitas.

28. Kalimat possitive and active 5 buah contoh kalimat!​


Jawaban:

Passive & Active Voice in the form of a Simple Present Tense :

[Active Voice] : Gembul eat fried rice in the moring. (Gembul makan nasi goreng pagi ini)

[Passive Voice] : Fried rice is ate by gembul in the morning. (Nasi goreng dimakan Gembul pagi ini)

Passive & Active Voice in the form of a Perfect Tense :  

[Active Voice] : Ridho has told me about his ucle. (Ridho bercerita padaku tentang pamannya)

[Passive Voice] : I have been told about Ridho’s uncle. (Aku diceritakan oleh Ridho tentang pamannya)

Passive & Active Voice in the form of a Past Continuous :

[Active Voice] : She was boiling the vegetables while Mr Dakir maked the grilled meat. (Dia sedang merebus sayuran saat Pak Dakir membuat daging panggang)

[Passive Voice] : The vegetables were being boiled by her while Mr Dakir maked the grilled meat. (Sayuran sedang direbus saat Pak Dakir membuat daging panggang)

Passive & Active Voice in the form of a Present Continuous Tense :

[Active Voice] : I am writting this love latter. (Aku menulis surat cinta ini)

[Passive Voice] : This love latter being written by me. (Surat cinta ini sedang ditulis olehku)

Penjelasan:

Kalimat Aktif (Active Voice)

Kalimat yang subjeknya menggunakan kata kerja atau sedang melakukan suatu tindakan, maka disebut sebagai kalimat aktif atau active voice. Kalimat aktif pada umumnya sangat mudah untuk dipahami.

Kalimat Pasif (Passive Voice)

Kalimat yang subjeknya diberi suatu tindakan ataupun diberi kata kerja, maka disebut kalimat pasif atau passive voice.

38. contoh dialog expressing politeness lengkap


Denny : So, today we will make a guava juice.
Arisa : All right. What do we need?
Denny : We need, of course, the main thing, a guava fruit, sugar, condensed milk, and crushed ice cubes.
Arisa : Okay. I've prepared all of it.
Denny : Then, let's begin! Can you pass  me the guava fruit, please?
Arisa : Here you are.
Denny : Thanks. While I wash and cut the fruit, could you please prepare the blender?
Arisa : Sure thing.
Denny : Well, I've cut the fruit. Now, we add the pieces into the blender. Um, will you add the condensed milk, please?
Arisa : Of course I will.
Denny : Thank you. Now, let's add 3 table spoon of sugar into the blender.
Arisa : Wow, it looks yummy.
Denny : Not yet, we still haven't blended it. Would you pass me the crushed ice cubes, please?
Arisa : Here you are. Be careful, it's cold.
Denny : Okay, now it's ready to blend. Wait a bit until it looks smooth.
Arisa : I can't wait! Yummy~
Denny : I think it's done. Could you prepare the glass, please?
Arisa : Okay, here it is.
Denny : Now, let's pour the guava juice into the glass. Ta-da! Our fresh, delicious guava juice is ready to be served!
Arisa : Yummy!
