In English, opinion and thoughts are rather different in their definition. 33. 1) pengertian agreeing with an opinion dan contohnya minimal 5?2) pengertian disagreeing with an opinion dan contohnya minimal 5?​with English


Jawaban:

1) **Agreeing with an Opinion:**

Agreeing with an opinion means expressing your support or alignment with a particular viewpoint or statement. Here are five examples:

a) "I completely agree with the idea that renewable energy sources are crucial for a sustainable future."

b) "I share the same opinion that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background."

c) "I'm of the same mind that stricter laws should be implemented to address environmental pollution."

d) "I'm on board with the argument that technology has significantly improved communication among people worldwide."

e) "I'm in favor of the viewpoint that cultural diversity enriches society and promotes tolerance."

2) **Disagreeing with an Opinion:**

Disagreeing with an opinion involves expressing your differing viewpoint or stance in response to a particular statement. Here are five examples:

a) "I respectfully disagree that standardized testing is the most effective measure of students' intelligence."

b) "I hold a different opinion that social media has contributed to increased feelings of isolation and loneliness."

c) "I'm of the opposite view that government intervention is not always the best solution to economic problems."

d) "I have a different perspective on the argument that violent video games directly lead to real-world aggression."

e) "I don't entirely agree that fast food should be entirely blamed for the rise in obesity; personal choices also play a role."

Remember that when expressing agreement or disagreement, it's important to do so respectfully and provide valid reasons or evidence to support your viewpoint.

Terjemahan :

1) **Agreeing with an Opinion:**

Setuju dengan pendapat berarti mengungkapkan dukungan atau kesesuaian dengan pandangan tertentu atau pernyataan. Berikut adalah lima contohnya:

a) "Saya sepenuhnya setuju dengan gagasan bahwa sumber energi terbarukan sangat penting untuk masa depan yang berkelanjutan."

b) "Saya memiliki pandangan yang sama bahwa pendidikan harus dapat diakses oleh semua orang, tanpa memandang latar belakang mereka."

c) "Saya sependapat bahwa undang-undang yang lebih ketat harus diterapkan untuk mengatasi polusi lingkungan."

d) "Saya sejalan dengan argumen bahwa teknologi telah secara signifikan meningkatkan komunikasi di antara orang-orang di seluruh dunia."

e) "Saya mendukung pandangan bahwa keragaman budaya memperkaya masyarakat dan mendorong toleransi."

2) **Disagreeing with an Opinion:**

Tidak setuju dengan pendapat melibatkan ekspresi pandangan atau sikap berbeda dalam merespons pernyataan tertentu. Berikut adalah lima contohnya:

a) "Saya dengan hormat tidak setuju bahwa pengujian standar adalah ukuran paling efektif untuk mengukur kecerdasan siswa."

b) "Saya memiliki pandangan yang berbeda bahwa media sosial telah berkontribusi pada peningkatan perasaan isolasi dan kesepian."

c) "Saya memiliki pandangan yang berlawanan bahwa intervensi pemerintah tidak selalu merupakan solusi terbaik untuk masalah ekonomi."

d) "Saya memiliki pandangan yang berbeda tentang argumen bahwa permainan video kekerasan secara langsung menyebabkan agresi dalam kehidupan nyata."

e) "Saya tidak sepenuhnya setuju bahwa makanan cepat saji sepenuhnya harus disalahkan atas peningkatan obesitas; pilihan pribadi juga berperan."

Ingatlah bahwa saat menyatakan persetujuan atau ketidaksetujuan, penting untuk melakukannya dengan hormat dan memberikan alasan atau bukti yang valid untuk mendukung pandangan Anda.

Jawaban:

1. Agreeing with an opinion (Setuju dengan pendapat)

Artinya setuju dengan pendapat seseorang.

5 contoh pengertian agreeing with an opinion dan contohnya

1. Pengertian: Setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan menyatakan hal yang sama.

Contoh: "I totally agree with you. The government should invest more in education to combat poverty."

2. Pengertian: Setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan memberikan alasan yang sama.

Contoh: "I agree with your opinion on stricter regulations for corruption. It is necessary to ensure transparency and accountability."

3. Pengertian: Setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan memberikan tambahan informasi yang relevan.

Contoh: "I agree with your point about the negative impact of corruption. It not only affects the economy but also undermines trust in the government."

4. Pengertian: Setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan memberikan contoh konkret yang mendukung pendapat tersebut.

Contoh: "I agree with you that poverty can lead to an increase in juvenile delinquency. Studies have shown that lack of access to education and opportunities can push young people towards criminal activities."

5. Pengertian: Setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan menawarkan solusi atau tindakan yang dapat diambil.

Contoh: "I agree with your concern about the rising corruption rate. One possible solution is to strengthen law enforcement agencies and implement stricter penalties for corrupt officials."

2. Disagreeing with an opinion (Tidak setuju dengan pendapat)

artinya tidak setuju dengan pendapat seseorang.

5 contoh pengertian disagreeing with an opinion dan contohnya

1. Pengertian: Tidak setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan memberikan alasan yang berbeda.

Contoh: "I respectfully disagree with your opinion on poverty. I believe that addressing the root causes, such as unequal distribution of resources, is more effective than providing temporary relief."

2. Pengertian: Tidak setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan menawarkan sudut pandang alternatif.

Contoh: "I understand your concern about corruption, but I disagree that stricter regulations are the only solution. I believe that promoting transparency and empowering citizens to hold officials accountable can also make a significant impact."

3. Pengertian: Tidak setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan menunjukkan kelemahan atau konsekuensi negatif dari pendapat tersebut.

Contoh: "I respectfully disagree with your suggestion to increase funding for education without addressing systemic issues. Without proper governance and efficient use of resources, increased funding alone may not lead to significant improvements."

4. Pengertian: Tidak setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan menawarkan pendekatan atau solusi alternatif.

Contoh: "I disagree with your perspective on juvenile delinquency. Instead of focusing solely on punishment, I believe that investing in preventive measures such as mentorship programs and access to recreational activities can help steer young people away from trouble."

5. Pengertian: Tidak setuju dengan pendapat seseorang dan mengajukan pertanyaan atau mengundang diskusi lebih lanjut.

Contoh: "I have a different opinion regarding corruption. Can you explain more about why you think stricter regulations are the most effective approach? I'd like to understand your perspective better."

38. apa pengertian giving opinion dalam bahasa iInggris? Dan Berikan contohnya


Opinion is a phrase or sentence consist of argument and reason about certain issue. Giving opinion is a sentence or expression that gives a reason to someone or other people about your personal/general point of view.

Pembahasan

Ketika akan memberikan opini atau pendapat mengenai suatu topik/isu ada beberapa kalimat ungkapan yang bisa kalian gunakan disini sebagai awalan. Contoh:

I think….I believe….According to me….In my view….In my opinion….From my perspective….From my point of view….Personally, I think….Some people say that….

Berikut adalah contoh cara memberikan opini berdasarkan situasi tertentu:

1. What do you think about the Jakarta’s MRT that will be launched soon on 2019?

Answer: I think it’s a great thing that finally the capital has its own effective and fast transportation system. Jakarta’s people will no longer have to struggle with traffic jams and crowded public transportation anymore.

2. How do you feel about the corruption in Indonesia?

Answer: From my perspective, it’s very sad that corruption has been one of the major problem in Indonesia which eventually causing poverty. I believe that Indonesia could be a better country one day, but together we as a nation should eradicate corruption as soon as possible.
