6. Please write biography about Kalimantan island/Borneo island.


Jawaban:

Kalimantan Island is an island in Indonesia that has not been widely explored by tourists but has many uniqueness and charms, including a rich cultural heritage. Kalimantan Island is famous for its tropical forests and has the most natural resources in Indonesia. Exotic flora and fauna are also offered by the land of Borneo.

In the beginning, the island was inhabited by the Dayak people as the original tribe of Kalimantan. Usually they live in the forest and live nomadically. However, at this time changes and the flow of modernization have brought some indigenous Dayak people to mingle with people from various ethnic groups in urban areas. The Dayak people live in long houses called Lamin or Umat Daarul. Usually a house is occupied by 50 people or more. There are many Dayak tribes and sub-tribes recorded on the island of Borneo.

The river plays a very important role in the communication and economy of its inhabitants. Most of the people live and are centered along the river flow including their activities and their houses. Kalimantan is divided into 4 provinces, namely East Kalimantan, South Kalimantan, Central Kalimantan, and also West Kalimantan. Each province has its own characteristics, language, natural potential, culture and uniqueness
