7. how does body language help us when communicating​


jawaban : to reveal our true feelings and to give our message more impact.

Penjelasan : untuk mengungkapkan perasaan kita yang sebenarnya dan memberikan dampak yang lebih besar pada pesan kita.

Jawaban:

Body language is a powerful communication tool that complements verbal communication, provides additional context and meaning, and facilitates effective and nuanced interaction with others.

Penjelasan:

Body language plays a significant role in communication by complementing and enhancing the messages we convey verbally. Here's how body language helps us when communicating:

1. Nonverbal expression: Body language includes facial expressions, gestures, posture, and eye contact, which can express emotions, attitudes, and intentions. It adds depth and nuance to our spoken words, helping to convey meaning beyond the literal message.

2. Enhancing understanding: Body language can help clarify and reinforce our verbal communication. It provides visual cues that aid in understanding the speaker's tone, emphasis, and intent. For example, a nod of agreement while saying "yes" reinforces the message and shows sincerity.

3. Conveying emotions: Body language can reveal emotions and feelings that may not be explicitly stated. Facial expressions, hand gestures, and body movements can convey happiness, sadness, excitement, or frustration, allowing others to better understand our emotional state.

4. Building rapport: Body language is crucial for establishing rapport and creating a connection with others. Open and relaxed postures, maintaining eye contact, and friendly gestures can contribute to a positive and engaging interaction.

5. Nonverbal cues in listening: Effective communication involves active listening. Body language cues such as nodding, leaning forward, and maintaining eye contact show attentiveness and interest, encouraging the speaker to continue expressing themselves.

6. Nonverbal feedback: Body language serves as a form of feedback in communication. It can indicate agreement or disagreement, engagement or disinterest, understanding or confusion. Observing others' body language helps us gauge their response and adjust our communication accordingly.

7. Cultural context: Body language is also influenced by cultural norms and can vary across different cultures. Being aware of cultural differences in body language helps us navigate cross-cultural communication and avoid misinterpretation.

Peran manajer adalah ....a. bugjeting, organizing, communicating,dan rekrutmenb. informasing, controlling, organizing,dan comunicatingC. communicating, planning, controlling,dan bugjetingd. planning, bagjeting, organizing,controlling, dan somunicatinge. planning, organizing, staffing, directing,dan controlling​


Jawaban:

e.planning, organizing, staffing, directing, dan controlling

Penjelasan:

Planning, Organizing, Motivating, Controlling, Evaluating merupakan fungsi manajemen menurut S. P. Siagian.

Pembahasan

Berikut adalah fungsi manajemen menurut S. P. Siagian:

Planning (perencanaan) yakni menentukan tujuan untuk kinerja organisasi dimasa depan serta memutuskan tugas dan penggunaan sumber daya yang diperlukan untuk mencapai tujuan tersebut.

Organizing (pengorganisasian) yakni mengalokasikan seluruh pekerjaan yang harus dilaksanakan antara kelompok kerja, menetapkan wewenang relatif serta tanggung jawab masing-masing individu atas komponen kerjadan menyediakan lingkungan kerja yang tepat.  

Motivating (motivasi) yakni pemberian inspirasi, semangat dan dorongan kepada bawahan agar bawahan melakukan kegiatan secara suka rela sesuai apa yang diinginkan oleh atasan.

Controlling (pengawasan) yakni mengawasi aktivitas karyawan, menentukan apakah organisasi dapat memenuhi target tujuannya, dan melakukan koreksi bila diperlukan serta menilai pelaksanaan kegiatan.

Evaluating (penilaian) merupakan fungsi untuk memberikan penilaian dalam berbagai kegiatan serta menilai sejauh mana usaha mencapai tujuan yang telah ditetapkan.
